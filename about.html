My Story

From a One-Way Ticket
to a Way of Life

It started with a one-way flight to Lisbon and a suitcase I packed in forty minutes. I had a vague plan, a shaky grasp of Portuguese, and absolutely no idea that I'd still be here — or rather, everywhere — five years later. What began as a sabbatical from a London desk job became something I can only describe as a complete reordering of priorities. I traded the commute for cobblestones, the office kitchen for tascas that don't have menus, and the certainty of a paycheque for the gloriously uncertain art of slow travel.

"I don't travel to escape life. I travel because this is my life."

Wander With Suzy is where I put it all down — the planning guides and the disasters, the budget hacks and the splurges, the meals I'll remember forever and the hostels I'm trying to forget. I write for the curious, the indecisive, and the chronically over-packed. I've navigated Bangkok's night markets at midnight, chased cherry blossoms across three Tokyo parks in one morning, and once got so lost in the Marrakech medina that a cat had to show me the way out. I'm not an expert; I'm just someone who keeps going, keeps taking notes, and keeps sharing the view.
